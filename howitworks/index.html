How it Works

Step One

Email us at info@primaryphoto.net with the following information:

  • Your name and phone number

  • What camera you're using

  • Class preferences (details)

  • What you'd like to learn

  • Preferred meeting time

Step Two

One of our instructors will contact you within one business day to discuss your lesson and accept payment.

Step Three

Once payment has been confirmed, you will receive an email within 24 hours. This email will have your name, contact info, seminar subject matter, instructors name, location and date of the seminar. If any of the information that appears in your confirmation email is incorrect, contact us ASAP.

That's it!

Meet your instructor at the scheduled date and time and get ready to start shooting like a pro! We always recommend you bring your camera or any pertinent equipment you want to learn, charged batteries (if applicable) and something to take notes with. After your lesson, be sure to tell us how we did by visiting us on Facebook or Yelp! Or get our photo tip-of-the-day by following us on Twitter.

Legal Stuff

For liability reasons, we cannot send an instructor to the private home of any student, nor can the student come to an instructors residence. We instead will send the instructor to meet the student at any convenient public location within a ten-mile radius of the downtown Portland area. The instructor reserves the right to refuse any location if they see it as a risk to the personal safety of themselves or the student. Any location refusals will be made known to the student in advance of the instruction date and with enough time for a new location to be selected.
